Barbell Back Squats

Overview

This exercise will get your booty popping like no other while letting you get massive gains in strength and volume. Place the bar on your traps and point your toes in an outward angle. Now go down like you're sitting in a chair and then stop at a 90 degree angle. Then go back up and repeat. You will feel this is in your glutes and quads.

Clamshell

Overview

This exercise is done by laying down and assuming a near fetal position. When you assume the position, you open up the leg that is not on the ground like a clam and then put it back. Repeat and you will feel it all in your glute.

Overview
