Romanian Deadlifts

Overview

A killer exercise for getting volume in your hamstrings only. Grab a pair of dumb bells and keep your legs bent slightly while having the weights in front of you. Keep your back straight and start like you are pulling something up from the ground.

Leg Press

Overview

Leg presses are a good way to get volume in your quads, glutes, and hamstrings. By altering the stance you strike up when putting your feet against the platform, you can rep it out and feel the burn more because you will be doing high volume sets.

Leg Extensions

Overview

This exercise requires you to have a leg extension machine where it focuses purely on quads.In a seated position, you can either do single leg or both legs at a time and raise up the weighted bar.

Goblet Squats

Overview

Using a dumb bell or a kettle bell, this exercise focuses mainly on the quads. By holding a dumb bell or a kettle bell in your hands and squatting, you really tear at your quads and feel the burn.
