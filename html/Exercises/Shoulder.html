Military Overhead Press

Overview

Starting off with a barbell. Be in a standing position and grip normally. Now push up so the barbell is over your head and then bring it back down slowly. Good for hitting all of your shoulders and giving it volume and strength.

Dumb Bell Lateral Raises

Overview

A good exercise for targeting the big head of your shoulders. It is what helps give you broader shoulders. Grabbing a dumbbell, you can lift it up laterally and it will let you feel a burn along the long head and make you have those cannonball shoulders to let you carry people on your shoulders.

Seated Overhead Presses

Overview

Similar to the military overhead press. Instead of standing, you sit down to take out you abs and reduce momentum. A good way to really isolate your shoulders and have increased stability. With dumb bells, it helps you fix instability and gets a good pump in.

Steps

Arnold Presses

Overview

A variation of the seated overhead press. This is Arnold Schwarzenegger's infamous variation where you assume the overhead press but keep your arms at a 90 degree angle first, you press, then bring it back to 90 degrees and bring your arms inwards and then back out and press again. It really gives your shoulders a burn while also working out a bit of your chest.
