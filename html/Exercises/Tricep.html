Dumbbell Kickbacks

Overview

A good tricep exercise to focus on one arm at a time for fixing muscle irregularity and instability. Put one leg on the bench and the other straight on the ground. Take a low weight dumb bell and have your arm behind you parallel to the ground. Now lower the dumb bell so it is now at a 90 degree angle and then bring it back up.

Cable Pulldowns

Overview

Pulldowns really allow you to focus purely on triceps and hit's the small and big head of your triceps. It is what allow you to get that volume and definition with enough reps. Grab the cable and have it extended out in an upside down v position and pull down all the way. The trick is to keep your elbows pointed in a perpendicular motion and still while your hands and the rest of your arms moving back up.

Dumbbell Incline Press

Overview

This focuses on form as well as fixing instability within your chest. Dumb bell incline press helps you work out form very well by sitting on a incline bench. lift the dumb bells up and then bring them down to a make your arms form a 90 degree angle and then push back up to straighten it.

Bench Dips

Overview

A good finisher for triceps. Place your hand behind your back and onto the side of a bench, have your upper body straight and legs straight with the heels of your feet only touching the ground. Now slowly lower yourself with your arms and you will feel the burn when going down and back up.
