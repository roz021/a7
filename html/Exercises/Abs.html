Crunches

Overview

Crunches are a great exercise that target the middle region of your abs.

Steps

  1. Lay flat on your back with knees bent feet flat on floor, hips width apart.
  2. Place hands behind your head so your thumbs are behind your ears.
  3. Tilt chin slightly & leave a few inches of space between chin & chest.
  4. Gently pull abs inward.
  5. Curl up in a forward motion so that head, neck, and shouder blades are off the the floor.
  6. Hold for a moment and then lower back down slowly.

Russian Oblique Twists

Overview

Russian Oblique twists are a great way to target your obliques while also hitting a bit of your middle abdominals.

Steps

  1. Lay at a 45 degree angle for both your legs and upper body on the floor in a v shape.
  2. Hold your arms out and clasp your hands together while clenching your abdominal muscles.
  3. With your arms extended out, twist your torso to the left and then to the right vice versa.
  4. For a challenge, try keeping your legs elevated but still in a v stance position

Situps

Overview

Similar to crunches but more focused on hitting the middle and upper abdominal region.

Steps

  1. Lay flat on the floor and assume an upside down v for your legs like you would for crunches.
  2. Squeeze your abdominal muscles and place your hands behind your head.
  3. Lift your head,neck, and upper body off the floor.
  4. Once your upper body is off the floor, hold for a moment and slowly go back down.

Decline Situps

Overview

A variation to the situp, focuses more on lower abdominals.

Steps

  1. Using a decline bench, hook your legs through the leg rests.
  2. place your whole body along the bench and place your hands on the back of your head.
  3. Lift your neck, head, and upper body as if you are doing a situp.
  4. Hold for a moment and go back down slowly.
