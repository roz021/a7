Crunches

Crunches are where you lie on the ground with your legs bent in an upside down v pose. Then you squeeze your abs and raise up a bit to get your upper body off the ground about 30 degrees and then go back down.

Russian Oblique Twists

Put your body in a sitting position with your upper body at an 45 degree angle and legs at an upside down v pose. Keep your arms close to your chest and twist in a left and right motion. You will feel a burn on the sides of your core.

Situps

Similar to crunches, sit down with your butt planted on the ground and legs in a upside down v position. this time, clench your abs and go all the way up until your chest touches your knees. Then go back down and that will be one rep.

Decline Situps

A slight variation to the situp. This time, using a decline bench, place your body and hook your legs through the leg rests and enact the situp position. This exercise focuses more on your lower abs.
