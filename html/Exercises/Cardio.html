Sprints

Overview

Sprints are good for training explosive speed, quads, and improving heart rate.

Steps

  1. Have your arms tucked to the side of your body and in a crounched forward motion.
  2. Push off the ground with your legs and have your arms cycle up and down while running.

Ellipticals

Overview

Known as the knee saving cardio since your feet don't pound on the ground.

Steps

  1. Find an elliptical machine and plant your feet on the designated foot areas.
  2. Turn on the machine and start pedaling.

Stairmasters

Overview

A good exercise to punish your legs and get your glutes and quads quivering.

Steps

  1. Find a stairmaster machine and turn it on.
  2. Place your feet on the stairs and start walking like you're going up stairs.

Rowing

Overview

Good workout for making you keep a steady tempo while also working out your upper back.

Steps

  1. Find a rowing machine and turn it on.
  2. Set your feet on the foot guards and strap up.
  3. Grab the handles and keep your back straight and pull back.
  4. After pulling back, lean forward with the handles in hand and repeat the rowing motion.
