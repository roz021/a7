Standing Calf Raises

Overview

Standing Calf Raises are a good way to grow your calves without placing too much pressure on your body.

Steps

  1. Grab a pair of dumb bells and grip them to your sides while keeping your body stiff.
  2. Lift up your heeels while keeping your toes on the ground.
  3. Hold for a moment while heels elevated in the air.
  4. Slowly bring back down to the ground and repeat.

Calf Leg Presses

Overview

Great way to grow calves with a heavier weight or lower weight for volume sets.

Steps

  1. Find a leg press machine and sit down on it.
  2. Plant your feet halfway where your from half your feet to toes are on the platform.
  3. Release the safeguards and slowly bring your knees in and hold for a moment.
  4. Push up with your toes till you feel your calves stretch.

Seated Calf Raises

Overview

Seated Calf raises are a good way to load weight without putting a strain on your arms.

Steps

  1. Adjust the weights to a comfortable or challenging weight and place your buttocks on the seat.
  2. Keep your upper body straght and your feet halfway planted on the foot rests.
  3. Pull the safeguard to the side and lift your legs up by using your toes and go all the way up and down slowly.

Farmer Walks on toes

Overview

Farmer Walks on Toes really make your calves burn because you're constantly engaging the muscle.

Steps

  1. Grab a set of dumb bells and have your arms to the side of your body
  2. Walk around only on your toes and do a circuit.
